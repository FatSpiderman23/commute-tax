Redundancy FAQs

How is statutory redundancy pay calculated?

You get half a week's pay for each year under 22, one week's pay for each year between 22-40, and one and a half week's pay for each year over 41. Weekly pay is capped at £643 in 2025/26.

Is redundancy pay taxable?

The first £30,000 of redundancy pay is tax-free. Any amount above £30,000 is subject to income tax but not National Insurance.

Can my employer pay more than statutory?

Yes — many employers offer enhanced redundancy pay above the statutory minimum. Check your employment contract or staff handbook for details.
